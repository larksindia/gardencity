GREENFIELD

Greenfield is a residential project located in Budigere cross, off Old Madras Road. Spread across 19.42 acres, this 18-storey project offers 1,588 apartments comprising of 2 & 3 BHKs ranging from 928 sq. ft. to 1330 sq. ft. Greenfield comes with all the modern amenities of a gated community. The Project is being developed as a joint venture between Gardencity Realty & Shriram Properties. Phase 1 of approximately 1.1 million sq. ft. is expected to be handed-over by October 2018.

ANEKAL

Planned at Manchanahalli Village, Attibelle Hobli, Anekal Taluk, Anekal is a residential project spread across 18 acres of land with estimated saleable area of 2 million sq. ft. The Project is being developed as a joint venture between Gardencity Realty & Shriram Properties. The Project aims to meet the affordable housing requirements of people working in Electronic city/Hosur Road and plans to be launched by September 2018.

ICONICA GRANDE

Iconica Grande is a residential villa project being developed at Madhurwada, Vishakapatnam. This project consists of 138 villas spread 17 acres and offers villas ranging from 3065 sq. ft. to 4675 sq. ft. Iconica Grande includes all the basic amenities of a gated community and is ideal for those who want to incorporate space and style to everyday living.
