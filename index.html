ABOUT US

We believe it isn't enough to make at first impressiom. Our dream is to make impressions that last. We are ICONICA Constructions.

We are a part of Gardencity Realty Pvt Ltd.(GPRL), a real estate builder and venkatalakshmi Holdings Pvt Ltd.,an investment company, which focuses on investing in real estate projects/companies.

Headquarted in Kalyan Nagar,Bangalore. we are currently planning and executing a varied range of projects - High-rise residential apartments, New generation homes, High-end commericial complexes, Recreational and Entertainment complexes, Hotels, Educational institutions, Hospital as well as Office spaces.

Using the very latest technology in the field of construction, Our vision is to deliver landmark projects that create long-lasting progress and economic growth.Our f=dream is to provide excellent world-class living and working enviornment. Leaving nothing but lasting impressions behind.

SKottu L Santosh – Director1

With a Master’s degree in Business Administration from the United Kingdom, Mr. Santosh is armed with over 7 years of domain experience in real estate. Renowned for his strategic thinking and planning, he serves as the Director of GRPL and is a part of the core management team. He advises the team on investments and creates strategic business plans that lead to success. Under his leadership, Garden City Realty has developed and delivered several landmark projects. One such project is Temple Bells, a collection of 342 green apartments at Rajininagar in Bangalore which was completed in 2009. In Hyderabad, as the chairman of Cyber City Developer, Mr. Krishna oversaw the launch of a 90-acre project called the Rainbow Vistas @Rock Garden. The Phase I of the project has seen the on-time delivery of over 448 apartments with another 1, 500 units currently being delivered in Phase II. The Phase III is under development with 3, 000 units in the planning stage.
